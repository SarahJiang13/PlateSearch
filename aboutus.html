Geroge Man

Founder of RPS

Geroge Man is the founder of RPS. When he wasa younger boy, he used to live with his father in a small rural country where animals were not regarded as important. Geroge was raised to believe that animals were just animals and did not hold any significance like human. When he was 5 he found a bead possum and kids poking the coprse, he felt bad which is why he soon after dedicated his whole life to ensure that animals were saved and treat just like a living person and not just some animal. Geroge is now 25 and still continues to keep this foundation going with as muhc power as he can. Geroge believes that there will be a day where there will be no need to spread awareness as poeple would have already known how important animals are.

History of RPS

RPS was founded on, Dec 12, 2017 with George and his 3 friends

Geroge, Kristen and Mark

Geroge, Kristen and Mark were great friends when they were younger and when Geroge foolishly suggested that they start an animal rescue group when drunk on day, they all complaied and hence started the development of the RPS. Not a lot of money could be put into the RPS and so they dropped the idea, that was until 2 years later when they reunited and decided that they were officially going to start the RPS group.


What is the RPS

Rescue Persuers Sanctuary

The RPS logo

The RPS is a foundation that does everything they can to save animals no matter what. We take great inicitive and responsibility when it comes to save animals. Our team is always on stand by whether it be at night or in the morning, we are always ready for a call to help save an animal's life. We ensure that all animals are given a chance of happiness and life. We take our work very seriously here at the RPS and is always open looking for new teammates and volunteers or our new adoption system.
