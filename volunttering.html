    Volunteering Donations Become part of the team

How to apply?

Here at RPS we love recieving notifications indicating someone is interested in volunteering and helping us. Please ensure that you have read through the conditions on being a volunteer and fully ablidge or is elligable before applying. Thanks and regards RPS team.

What will I have to do?

Depending on what roles you choose to volunteer to because every role varies with what needs to be done.

Most roles are collecting money, looking after rescued animals and helping pick up calls as well as customer service.

What are my responsiibilitis

You would need to unsure that you look out for youself all the time as your safety in not our responsibility but transport and material is all provided by us.

Looking after some hurt animals and catering to their minor injuries may also be a task placed on you if we get held back and don't have enough staff to tend to animals so please ensure that you are prepared for that posiblilty,

Age requirements

Unfortunetly we are unable to accept all people, age requirements vary depending on the role you are applying for. A warning and age rirement notification will be given before applying.

Id or evidence that you reach the age requirement needed for the role must be presented to a team member before you start.

What are the benefits?

Volunteering does you community well and it help save lives as animal lives are also human lives.

If you have interest in joining the team, excess volunteering will certainly boost your chances with getting accepted.

What do I get for volunteering?

Once you have completed your task, you will be given an official certificate from us personally and recieve a free meal that will be provided by us.

It could also essentially boost your chances in future when it comes to looking for a job or applying for another rescue group.
