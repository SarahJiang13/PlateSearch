Become part of the team

The RPS is not only a team but a family, everyone takes a huge role and puts a great amount of inicitive when it comes to getting the RPS working and getting those animals into safe, loving homes.

How to apply?

To apply you will need to send an email through along with the role that you would like to participate in.

Please attach all doccument =s that you believe is relevent and we will respond within 1-3 days about how your application went throuhg.

How much will I get paid?

A cheesy way to say but you not only get paid in money but also a good feeling knowing that you have saved a life.

All of that aside, you get paid around $20 per hour.

What am I required to do?

Depending on the role that you choose, requirments are certainly not something that we take big though into but it does depend on the role you are trying to apply for.

Some roles may involve some dangerous tasks which is why we ask you to ensure that you want to do the role before applying.
